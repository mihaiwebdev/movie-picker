Movie Picker is an app that helps you choose movies on your favorite streaming platform based on your preferences and mood.

Yes, the app is completely free to use. You can sign up for free to unlock all the features and start using it right away.

No account is necessary to use the app. However, creating one unlocks additional features.

Movie Picker generates suggestions based on a combination of vote count and average vote ratings from The Movie Database (TMDb). This ensures that you receive recommendations that are both highly rated and widely popular, giving you the best possible matches.

The app is mobile friendly and can be easly used on all devices.

No, you can't watch full movies on the platform, the purpose of the app is to help you choose a movie in seconds to watch on your favorite streaming platform.

We get the movies from the TMDb API, which is a free API that provides information about movies and TV shows.
